About The Company

BUILDING INDIA'S
INFRASTRUCTURE
FUTURE

Prayag Enterprises is a registered industrial maintenance and supply solutions enterprise based in Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh. We specialize in delivering end-to-end maintenance support, infrastructure upkeep, and wholesale supply services to industrial, telecom, power, and healthcare sectors.

Our operations are underpinned by verified field experience with leading organizations including SSS (Secured Security Solutions Pvt. Ltd.) and direct maintenance exposure with Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd. Our wholesale division has established supply partnerships with Uniclan Healthcare Pvt. Ltd. and Uniclan Hygiene Pvt. Ltd. — subsidiaries associated with DOMS Industries.

Spearheaded by our Founder & CEO, Mr. Indra Kumar Pandey, Prayag Enterprises is steadily expanding its operational footprint with a clear vision for industrial security solutions and smart infrastructure.
